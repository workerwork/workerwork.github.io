2. 编译选项

1.make install-dep #install software dependencies
2.make wipe(wipe-release) #wipe all products
3.make build(build-release) #build binaries
4.make install-ext-deps #install external development dependencies(about dpdk)
5.make pkg-deb #build DEB packages
